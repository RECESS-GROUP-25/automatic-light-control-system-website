Light is a form of energy, that stimulates sight and makes objects visible. Light, mostly electrical light, plays an extremely important role in today’s modern society, as it is an important form of energy that allows humans to live the convenient life they currently live. One of the obvious uses of light energy in one’s daily life is to provide light, allowing one to see their surroundings and avoid living and working in the dark. Electric light is the most used form of artificial light, and is found to be used in almost everything, from providing interior lighting for building and houses, to providing exterior light for nighttime but with resent load shadings and unplanned power outages in the country it becomes clear that the energy crisis have not been resolved to its full. As domestic houses still uses electricity without caution, high energy consumption remains a bigger issue with one being light consumption.

Light Management For Everyone

The whole aim of our system is to allow people to use lights not only where it is needed but also when it is needed thus lighting controls should be used:

  • to make maximum use of daylighting
  • to reduce light levels when the task changes
  • to turn off lighting when light is not needed
